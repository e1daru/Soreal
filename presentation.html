The Problem

Legal Work Is Crushing SMBs

  • ✕ Contracts, compliance docs, and agreements pile up — unstructured and unsearchable
  • ✕ Lawyers charge $300–500/hr for manual review — SMBs can't afford it at scale
  • ✕ Generic AI chatbots hallucinate — no source traceability, no confidence
  • ✕ Relationships between parties, clauses, and obligations are invisible in flat text

Our Solution

Under the Hood

Tech Stack & Key Features

🗄️ SurrealDB

Graph + relational + vector in one DB. HNSW indexes for similarity search. SurrealQL for graph traversal.

🤖 LangGraph + LangChain

StateGraph orchestration. 7 custom tools. Conditional routing. MemorySaver persistent state.

🧠 Ollama (Llama 3.1)

Fully local LLM — no data leaves your machine. Critical for legal confidentiality.

Why This Matters for Legal

  • ✓ Source traceability — every answer points to the exact document chunk
  • ✓ Automated extraction — entities & relations extracted and loaded in one deterministic pass
  • ✓ Evolving knowledge — graph grows as new documents are analyzed or new facts emerge
  • ✓ 100% local — confidential legal docs never leave the machine
  • ✓ Relationship discovery — surface hidden connections across parties, clauses, and obligations
  • ✓ Resumable workflows — pause review, come back later, pick up exactly where you left off
